LEARN DAVAO

Learn Davao is a web application that helps spread reliable information to Davaoenos to avoid the belief of fake news about the pandemic and the vaccine. The web application aims to help prevent the rise of COVID 19 cases by dispersing information and awareness.

What is Learn Davao?

Feature

Davao Learn will provide timely updates regarding the city's COVID-19 status, such as: Number of cases, High-risk areas, new implementations and, additional health tips and guide.

Reliable Source

To avoid the spread of fake news, we make sure that the data provided is from the City Government

Navigation

Davao Learn is solely for Davao City, it is designed for easy navigation such as searching specific baranggay or any information, we also aim to give users grapic presentations of data for easy understanding.
